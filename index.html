About Piximi

Computer scientists and biologists from four institutes (ETH Zurich, Biological Research Centre Szeged, FIMM Helsinki and Broad Institute) are coming together November 18 - 24 in Boston to hack the next generation cell classifier for the biological community.
Beautiful, easy-to-use, open-source and with deep learning support!

What are we developing?

Open source and community-driven

In this hackathon, we are designing the future of Advanced Cell Classifier and CellProfiler Analyst. Our goal is to build easy-to-use machine learning tools for the biological community. Build on tensorflow.js and react, we are aiming to design a fast and beautiful web-based classifier. Your data never leaves the browser.
